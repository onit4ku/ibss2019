International Bioinformatics Software School 2019

Registration and Submission

The workshop is open to graduate students and specializing scientists. The researchers are encouraged to submit their unpublished contributions that illustrate original research results or surveying works related to the areas of bioinformatics and data analysis in agriculture and healthcare.

IBSS'19 will present the Award of the Best Poster and the Best Presentation at the end.

Registration:

  • Download Application Form doc format and send it to: smbi.maroc@gmail.com

Membership:

  • Download Membership Form doc format and send it to: smbi.maroc@gmail.com

Submission:

  • Poster
    1. Authors are invited to write their poster contributions in English, using the following template instructions: Abstract instructions
    2. Applications should be submitted directly to: smbi.maroc@gmail.com

  • Paper
    1. Authors are invited to write their paper contributions in English, using the following template instructions: Paper instructions
    2. Applications should be submitted directly to: https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=IBSS2019

  • Authors are invited to submit original unpublished manuscripts.
  • Accepted papers will be published in the proceedings and submitted to: https://www.scitechnol.com/applied-bioinformatics-computational-biology.php
  • For more Informations contact : smbi.maroc@gmail.com

Deadlines

  • Abstract submision: February 5th, 2019.
  • Paper submission: February 8th, 2019.
  • Notification of Acceptance: February 15th, 2019.
  • Selected applicants will then be asked to confirm their participation and pay the registration fees.
